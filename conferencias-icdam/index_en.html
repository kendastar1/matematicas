Information

Characteristics

In the mathematical event it is focused on a variety of topics, from number theory to geometry, passing through mathematical analysis, statistics, topology and many other fields.

In the mathematical event is organized by the Simon Bolivar University.

In the mathematical event, it focuses on the dissemination of mathematical research, it also focuses on mathematics education, on the development of skills, as well as on knowledge sharing and collaboration.

At the math event they can have a significant impact on the math community, from promoting new ideas and collaborations to networking and generating career opportunities.

In the mathematical event it may result in the publication of proceedings or scientific articles that document the results of the research presented in the event.

In the mathematical event you will have conferences, workshops, colloquiums, competitions, among others.

Authorization for acts of publication of documents in Open Access

IOP Conference Series operates on a publishing release, where authors retain copyright to their papers and no longer need to sign and submit copyright assignment forms. Any author wishing to publish in the IOP Conference Series must agree to the terms of the release, and by submitting a paper for publication, it is assumed that all authors agree to these terms.

The terms and conditions of the authorization are available at línea.

Where to send your work

Manuscripts, prepared according to the author's guidelines, should be sent to Oscar Valbuena Prada by email diasdematematicaaplicada@unisimonbolivar.edu.co by August 21, 2022. Please, the email subject should be [Paper_IX_ICDAM_Corresponding Author Last Name].

How to organize your work

The IOP Conference Series uses author-supplied PDF files for all print and online publications. Authors are asked to prepare their papers using Microsoft Word or LaTeX, according to the journal guidelines and templates, and then convert these files to PDF. Full instructions on how to prepare the article, along with templates, can be found on the IOP Conference Series.

It is important to ensure that when you submit your article, it is in its final form, ready for publication, and has been fully reviewed. IOP does not review documents and will not send proof of authorship prior to publication. have Note that post-post changes are generally not allowed , unless there are exceptional circumstances, so make sure your document has been checked for errors.

What you need to provide

The PDF file of the paper: When preparing the paper, the author must make sure to follow the instructions, but at a minimum:

Use European A4 size paper with a minimum margin of 25mm.

Include an abstract of the article.

Do not add any page numbers, headers, or footers to the article

Any media file you want to post online:

JPCS encourages authors to submit multimedia files to enhance the online versions of the published research articles.
